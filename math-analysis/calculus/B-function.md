- $B(a,b)=\int_0^1 t^{a-1}(1-t)^{b-1}dt$
- 联系[[calculus/gamma]]
  - $B(a,b)=\Gamma(a)\Gamma(b)/\Gamma(a+b)$
  - $B(1,1)=1$
- 通过[[integral-by-parts]]理解
  - $B(2,3)=\int_0^1t^1(1-t)^2dt=\int_0^1t^{0}(1-t)^3/3dt=1!2!/4!=\Gamma(2)\Gamma(3)/\Gamma(5)$
