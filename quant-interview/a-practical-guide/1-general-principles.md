# five principles
- broad knowledge base
  - 照顾不同背景
  - 体现个人能力
  - basic understanding，而不是深入理解（PhD thesis?）
- practice your interview skills
  - 第一印象重要，好好表达自己！
- listen carefully
  - 可以重问
  - 可以问线索
- speak your mind
  - 也不要太trivial
  - 关注key concepts
- make reasonable assumptions
  - explain your reasonable assumptions to get immediate feedback
